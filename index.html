How LyDia works

Model

Team strength (Pythagorean + last-10 form), home field, and a starting-pitcher ERA adjustment, run every morning against the day's real slate.

Market

Every pick is checked against the market's no-vig probability. No edge over the price means no pick — discipline is the product, not volume.

Accountability

Every preview is archived and every pick is graded the next day on the public Results page. Wins and losses alike — nothing is deleted.
