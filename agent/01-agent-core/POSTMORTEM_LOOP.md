# Postmortem Loop

Every completed slate must produce learning.

## Grade three things separately

1. Result: win/loss/push
2. Price: did we beat the closing line?
3. Process: was the reasoning good?

## Good process / bad result

A loss can still be a good pick when:

- The edge was real.
- The price was good.
- The pick beat the close.
- The game lost due to normal variance.

## Bad process / good result

A win can still be a bad pick when:

- The line was stale.
- The market moved against us.
- The pick relied on bad assumptions.
- The reasoning ignored major information.
